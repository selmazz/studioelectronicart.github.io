Destination 3- Fear

If you don't like a scare, retreat now! This destination is where all of your darkest fears lurk. The dark, the deep, the hiding, the watching. Ponder your fears and question whether they are rational. Why do you fear it? Should you?

If your fear is conronting your fears, head to another destination.
