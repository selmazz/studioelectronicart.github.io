RUN AWAY


You get up and leave but you soon realise he’s following you! You pick up the pace and eventually break into a sprint! Leo does the same and boy can that Renaissance/Titanic actor move! You’re pushing and running into folks across your path until you realise there’s only one place you can lose someone - The Architecture Building! You give it all you got with Leo just behind you, dashing through the door and up the steps. You run and scurry until you’re sure you’re safe. Eventually you see him shrug and lose interest. Once he leaves you take a breather and decide it’s best to get to your tutorial but wait! How in the world do you get out of here? Within a minute you realise you’re totally lost with no hope of escape. You are completely doomed!
