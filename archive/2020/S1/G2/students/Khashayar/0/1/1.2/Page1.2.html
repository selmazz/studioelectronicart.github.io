SAY HI

Leo finally gets here but something’s not right, he has two sharp fangs, a pair of ghastly eyes and the stench of undeath. Oh no he really is a vampire and of course he’s hungry for art as he pounces and with a single touch sucks all your artistic talent away! No wonder he painted the Mona Lisa, he’s an art-sucking vampire and you’re his latest victim. With all your art talent gone you’re now a basic pleb, how can you possibly attend your tutorial now? You are completely doomed!
