HOME

When we speak of Vietnam's gastronomy, we tend to think of pho (rice noodle), banh mi (sandwich), goi cuon (fresh spring rolls), cha gio (fried spring rolls), and ca phe (coffee), yet these haven't barely scratched the surface (over 150 dishes). This is why I created 'Vietnamese Cuisine' as an attempt to present some other amazing Vietnamese dishes that people should try if they get the chance. I hope you will have a wonderful time browsing this mini website, and maybe someday we can even have a meetup with food tour in my beautiful home country Vietnam.

Cheers,
Ti Nhong
