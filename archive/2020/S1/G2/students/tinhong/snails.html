SNAILS

Vietnamese loves to consume molluscs: from the little white clams to the enormous garlic snails. These are commonly served as snack food that can be share among friends and family. Depending on the types, they can be stir-fried, boiled, or grilled. Other ingredients can be added to enhance the flavours such as margarine, lemongrass, basils, chillies, shallots, or ground peanuts. The dishes are then served with the sauces from the cooking process or with other dipping sauces.
