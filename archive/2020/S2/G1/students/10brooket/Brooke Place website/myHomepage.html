KARIJINI NATIONAL PARK ADVENTURE

A photographic exploration focused on capturing the beauty of the natural in western Australia. All photos were taken in July 2020 in Karajini during in the COVID19 pandemic. A period in which all Australians were unable to travel internationally and were forced to go on holidays in there own state and get to know the beauty in the diversity of the Western Australian landscape
