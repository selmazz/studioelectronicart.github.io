you are not alone

Quarantine has been a tough time for many people I know. Without seeing friends, with the pressure to still be productive and the rapid change in news and everyday life, our mental health has been under stress. Fortunately, we have the ability to stay connected thanks to the internet.
It is important to be aware that despite being in isolation, you will never be alone.
