                                                                                      I made art...

 

This work is an exploration of the distribution of my work.

I wanted to utilise the versatility and accessability of QR codes to encourage viewers to visit my archive.

None of the QR codes ended up being viable, but it provides a means to show the ideas and intent behind the use of the codes.

The images above were created using a carved lino block which was printed on paper using acrylic paint.

The same lino block was also printed on fabric to form patches that can be sewn onto clothing.
