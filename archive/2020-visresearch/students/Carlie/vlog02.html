FIRST CARD The Hanged Man is a card of suspended animation, this man tells us that he is being restricted by some force in his life.It represents un-fulfillment and a lack of action. It also suggests that you are clinging onto something that is not fulfilling you. Whatever you are waiting for, when the Hanged Man appears, his message is, you need to let go and move.
SECOND CARD When the Wheel of Fortune arrives in your day, it is a very optimistic wave of Jupiter's magic wand. It is always a good omen to see the Wheel of Fortune, it is a reminder that the planet of luck Jupiter is on your side. This is not something that you have to do, and no action is required on your part. And expect things to happen quickly when it does!
THIRD CARD When the Justice card appears in a reading, the message is that Legal matters will go your way. This card is one that represents harmony and good grace.
FINAL CARD When the 8 of Pentacles appears in your Tarot reading, all of your hard work is about to pay off. This is a card of committed labour, a dedicated mindset to your ultimate goal When this card appears, know that your own personal success you have been toiling after is in the works, and will manifest in due time.
